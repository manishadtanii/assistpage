FAQ

Everything US Business Owners Ask First

Is $9/hr really the starting price what's the catch? +
No catch. $9/hr is our genuine starting rate for general admin and scheduling VAs on part-time engagements. Specialized skills (social media, real estate, eCommerce) start at $12/hr. We don't lock you into minimums or add hidden fees. You pay only for hours worked, and your invoice shows exactly what was done.
How is this different from hiring on Upwork or Fiverr? +
On Upwork, YOU do all the vetting, managing, and replacing. If your VA disappears, that's your problem. GlocalAssist handles matching, vetting, backup coverage, and quality oversight. We're a managed service you get the talent without the HR headache.
Will my VA actually work during US business hours? +
Yes this is non-negotiable for us. Every VA we place covers US business hours aligned to your timezone (EST, CST, MST, or PST). You get real-time Slack/email responses during your workday, not delayed overnight replies.
What if my VA isn't a good fit? +
We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your VA for any reason, we'll replace them at zero cost. No paperwork, no fee. We also check in at Day 7 and Day 30 to catch any friction early.
Is my business data safe? Do you sign NDAs? +
Absolutely. We sign an NDA before any files, logins, or sensitive data are shared. All work happens through encrypted, access-controlled channels. For healthcare clients, we follow HIPAA-aware protocols. Your data belongs to you we treat it that way.
Can I give my VA access to my existing tools (Gmail, QuickBooks, CRM, etc.)? +
Yes your VA integrates into your existing stack. No migration, no new software required. We work in Gmail, Outlook, Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, QuickBooks, Asana, ClickUp, and 100+ other tools.
What's the minimum commitment? +
No minimum commitment on the Starter plan. Professional and Team plans work best month-to-month. You can pause, scale, or cancel with 7 days' notice. We believe good work earns long-term relationships not lock-in contracts.
How quickly can I really start? +
Most clients submit a form on Monday and have their VA working by Wednesday. Our record is 19 hours from submission to first task completed. Onboarding is entirely handled by us you just approve the match and show up for the intro call.
